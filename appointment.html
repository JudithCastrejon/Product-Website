Policies do not skip!

Please read before booking an appointment

COVID-19

  • Bring a mask to their appointment, this is mandatory. If you do not have a mask, I will ask you to reschedule..
  • Keep mask on at all times, no exception.
  • Let me know if you are planning to bring anyone with you. Limit of 2 guests per customer.
  • Cancel your appointment if you do not feel good, or believe that you have been exposed to COVID-19, we can always reschedule your appointment!

General Policies

  • I do not require a deposit, so you can just book your appointment.
  • Please arrive at least 5 minutes before your appointment
  • Please let me know if you are running more than 10 minutes late.
  • Your appointment will be cancelled after being 20 minutes late.❌
  • Please notify me at least 24 hours in advance if you plan on cancelling your appointment.
  • I accept cash, venmo, and cash-app.
  • Please arrive with some idea of what you would like to get done, so we can get started right away.
  • If you would prefer to have a silence appointment, please let me know. I want you to be as comfortable as possible.
